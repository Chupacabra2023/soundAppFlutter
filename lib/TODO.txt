Interface: Offer a guided tour upon first launch. The user may want to use the sounds you provide, but they may also want to use their own sounds without having to delete yours. Let them create their own categories, fill their list with their own MP3s, and explain that they can make multiple selections, choose colors, etc. It should also be possible to restart the entire system via a button in the settings.

System: Quick switching between two audio devices. The goal is for a user to be able to test the music they’re about to play through headphones without the entire audience hearing it. When they toggle a button, the sound plays through the connected speaker. When they toggle the button the other way, the sound plays through the headphones.

Statistics: Tracking which features are used could help you improve your app. Be mindful of legal requirements—in my country, for example, you must obtain user consent to do this, and by default, if the user hasn’t provided consent, the feature must be disabled.
